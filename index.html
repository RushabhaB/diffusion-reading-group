About

This reading group is focused on building a fundamental understanding of diffusion models. We will work through the core theory from the ground up, so a basic understanding of probability theory is expected as a prerequisite.

The group is geared towards graduate students who either aim to apply diffusion models in their own domain research or are interested in working on diffusion models directly. Whether you're looking to leverage these techniques for your specific application area or contribute to advancing the field itself, this reading group is for you.
